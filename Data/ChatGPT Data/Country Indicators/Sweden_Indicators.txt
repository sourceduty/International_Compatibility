
Political Factors (P)

International Agreements Participation
- Member of NATO since 2024, actively involved in climate initiatives and international trade agreements.

Political Stability Index
- High level of political stability with robust democratic governance.

Corruption Perception Index
- Among the least corrupt countries globally.

Economic Factors (E)

GDP per Capita
- Approximately $60,000 USD.

Trade Openness Index
- Highly open to international trade, with strong participation in EU trade agreements.

Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) Inflows
- Substantial FDI, especially in technology, manufacturing, and renewable energy.

Social Factors (S)

Education Index
- High education index with a strong emphasis on quality education and high literacy rates.

Healthcare Quality Index
- High-quality, comprehensive healthcare services.

Human Development Index (HDI)
- Very high HDI, reflecting excellent outcomes in health, education, and income.

Cultural Factors (C)

Cultural Proximity Index (e.g., Hofstede's Cultural Dimensions)
- High individualism, low power distance, high indulgence.

Language Similarity Index
- Swedish is closely related to other Scandinavian languages.

Cross-Cultural Communication Adaptability
- High adaptability, supported by a diverse population and inclusive society.

Environmental Factors (Env)

Environmental Performance Index (EPI)
- Leader in environmental performance, excelling in air quality, biodiversity, and sustainability.

Carbon Footprint per Capita
- Relatively low carbon footprint for a developed country.

Renewable Energy Usage Percentage
- Significant portion of energy from renewable sources, with plans to increase capacity.
