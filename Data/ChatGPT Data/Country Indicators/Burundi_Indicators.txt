
Political Factors (P)

International Agreements Participation
- Burundi is involved in various international agreements, including efforts to negotiate bilateral labor migration agreements with Gulf States and other destination countries. These agreements aim to protect migrant workers and combat human trafficking. Burundi is also a member of the East African Community (EAC), participating in regional initiatives to enhance economic and social development.

Political Stability Index
- Burundi faces significant political instability, highlighted by internal conflicts and governmental challenges. The political environment is further complicated by ongoing violence and socio-economic issues, affecting overall stability.

Corruption Perception Index
- Transparency International ranks Burundi low on the Corruption Perception Index, indicating high levels of corruption. This remains a significant challenge for the country's governance and public trust.

Economic Factors (E)

GDP per Capita
- As of 2023, Burundi's GDP per capita is approximately $270, one of the lowest globally. The economy is primarily based on agriculture, with significant contributions from coffee and tea exports.

Trade Openness Index
- Burundi has a low trade openness index, reflecting limited integration into the global economy. Efforts are ongoing to improve the business climate and boost economic growth through various reforms.

Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) Inflows
- Burundi continues to attract minimal foreign direct investment due to political instability and regulatory challenges. The government, with support from international organizations, is working to create a more favorable environment for investment.

Social Factors (S)

Education Index
- Burundi has made progress in education, with a literacy rate of 68.4% as of 2017. The government allocates about 20% of its national budget to education, though significant disparities exist in access to quality education across different regions.

Healthcare Quality Index
- The healthcare system in Burundi faces severe challenges, particularly in rural areas. Access to quality healthcare services is limited, and the government is striving to improve healthcare infrastructure and services.

Human Development Index (HDI)
- Burundi ranks low on the Human Development Index, reflecting significant challenges in health, education, and income levels. Continued efforts are needed to address these socio-economic disparities and improve overall living standards.

Cultural Factors (C)

Cultural Proximity Index
- Burundi's cultural dimensions reflect a society with high power distance and collectivism, influenced by its traditional values and ethnic diversity. These cultural traits significantly impact social interactions and organizational behavior within the country.

Language Similarity Index
- The official languages of Burundi are Kirundi, French, and English, facilitating communication and cultural exchange within the region and with international partners. This linguistic diversity supports cross-cultural adaptability.

Cross-Cultural Communication Adaptability
- Burundi's multicultural society and emphasis on education contribute to adaptability in cross-cultural communication. The population's exposure to diverse cultures supports effective international interactions.

Environmental Factors (Env)

Environmental Performance Index
- Burundi faces significant environmental challenges, including deforestation and soil erosion. The government is implementing policies to improve environmental sustainability and manage natural resources effectively.

Carbon Footprint per Capita
- Burundi's carbon footprint per capita is low, reflecting its developing economy and limited industrial activities. Efforts are being made to increase the use of renewable energy and promote environmental conservation.

Renewable Energy Usage Percentage
- The usage of renewable energy in Burundi is growing, with investments in hydropower and solar energy projects. These initiatives aim to diversify the energy mix and promote sustainability.
