
Political Factors (P)

International Agreements Participation
- Active participant in international agreements like the Paris Agreement and the Kyoto Protocol.

Political Stability Index
- Stable parliamentary democracy with regular elections and peaceful power transfers.

Corruption Perception Index
- Faces challenges with corruption; ongoing efforts to improve transparency and governance.

Economic Factors (E)

GDP Per Capita
- Approximately $7,000 USD.

Trade Openness Index
- Moderately open to international trade with significant regional trade agreements.

Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) Inflows
- Increasing FDI, particularly in tourism and infrastructure sectors.

Social Factors (S)

Education Index
- Allocates around 5.7% of GDP to education; high literacy rate and school life expectancy.

Healthcare Quality Index
- Comprehensive healthcare system with good health outcomes.

Human Development Index (HDI)
- High HDI, reflecting progress in health, education, and income levels.

Cultural Factors (C)

Cultural Proximity Index (e.g., Hofstede's Cultural Dimensions)
- Shares cultural similarities with other Caribbean nations; high degree of collectivism.

Language Similarity Index
- English is the official language, facilitating communication and cultural exchange.

Cross-Cultural Communication Adaptability
- High adaptability due to diverse cultural background and prevalence of English.

Environmental Factors (Env)

Environmental Performance Index (EPI)
- Faces environmental challenges; efforts to improve through international agreements.

Carbon Footprint Per Capita
- Relatively low carbon footprint.

Renewable Energy Usage Percentage
- Committed to increasing renewable energy share through solar, wind, and hydroelectric power development.
