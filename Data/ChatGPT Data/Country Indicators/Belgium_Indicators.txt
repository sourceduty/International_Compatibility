
Political Factors (P)

International Agreements Participation
- Belgium is actively involved in numerous international agreements, including the Artemis Accords, which outline best practices for responsible behavior in space exploration. Additionally, Belgium participates in various bilateral and multilateral treaties covering a wide range of areas such as trade, environmental protection, and human rights.

Political Stability Index
- Belgium maintains a relatively stable political environment, supported by a strong democratic system. However, it faces challenges such as regional tensions between the Flemish and Walloon communities, which can impact political stability.

Corruption Perception Index
- According to Transparency International, Belgium ranks relatively well in terms of low corruption levels. The country has strong legal frameworks and institutions to combat corruption, contributing to its favorable ranking.

Economic Factors (E)

GDP per Capita
- As of 2023, Belgium's GDP per capita is approximately $53,000, reflecting its status as a high-income country with a diversified economy driven by sectors such as manufacturing, services, and technology.

Trade Openness Index
- Belgium has a high trade openness index, facilitated by its strategic location in Europe and active participation in international trade. The country is a major exporter, with top exports including chemicals, machinery, and pharmaceuticals.

Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) Inflows
- Belgium attracts substantial FDI, particularly in sectors like financial services, technology, and pharmaceuticals. The favorable investment climate and robust regulatory framework support these inflows.

Social Factors (S)

Education Index
- Belgium has a well-developed education system with a high literacy rate. Significant government investment in education at all levels ensures quality educational outcomes, contributing to the country's social and economic development.

Healthcare Quality Index
- The healthcare system in Belgium provides high-quality and comprehensive medical services. Ongoing investments in healthcare infrastructure ensure accessible and effective care for all citizens.

Human Development Index (HDI)
- Belgium ranks high on the Human Development Index, reflecting strong performance in health, education, and income levels. Continuous efforts to improve social services and economic stability help maintain this high ranking.

Cultural Factors (C)

Cultural Proximity Index
- Belgium's cultural dimensions, influenced by its diverse linguistic and regional communities, reflect a society with moderate individualism and high power distance. These cultural traits impact social interactions and organizational behavior within the country.

Language Similarity Index
- Belgium has three official languages: Dutch, French, and German. This multilingualism facilitates communication and cultural exchange with neighboring countries, enhancing international cooperation.

Cross-Cultural Communication Adaptability
- Belgium's multicultural society and robust education system contribute to strong adaptability in cross-cultural communication. The population's exposure to diverse cultures supports effective international interactions.
