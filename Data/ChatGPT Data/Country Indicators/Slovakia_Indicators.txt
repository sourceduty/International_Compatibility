
Indicators for Slovakia

Political Factors (P)

1. International Agreements Participation:
   - Slovakia is a member of various international organizations including the United Nations, European Union, NATO, World Trade Organization, and others. It has signed numerous international treaties and agreements since gaining independence in 1993.

2. Political Stability Index:
   - According to the World Bank's Worldwide Governance Indicators, Slovakia's political stability is measured at 0.47 (2022), indicating moderate stability.

3. Corruption Perception Index:
   - In 2022, Slovakia scored 49 out of 100 on Transparency International's Corruption Perception Index, ranking 50th out of 180 countries.

Economic Factors (E)

1. GDP per Capita:
   - The GDP per capita in Slovakia was approximately $19,627 USD in 2022.

2. Trade Openness Index:
   - Slovakia's trade openness, which measures the sum of exports and imports as a percentage of GDP, was about 183% in 2022, reflecting a highly open economy.

3. Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) Inflows:
   - In 2022, Slovakia received around $2.8 billion USD in FDI inflows.

Social Factors (S)

1. Education Index:
   - Slovakia's education index, part of the Human Development Index (HDI), was 0.791 in 2022, indicating a high level of educational attainment.

2. Healthcare Quality Index:
   - The healthcare index for Slovakia was approximately 64.7 out of 100 in 2022, indicating moderate quality healthcare services.

3. Human Development Index (HDI):
   - Slovakia's HDI in 2022 was 0.860, placing it in the very high human development category.

Cultural Factors (C)

1. Cultural Proximity Index:
   - Based on Hofstede's cultural dimensions, Slovakia scores high in power distance (104), individualism (52), masculinity (110), uncertainty avoidance (92), and long-term orientation (77), reflecting a culture that values hierarchy, individual achievements, and stability.

2. Language Similarity Index:
   - Slovak is the official language, and its closest linguistic relative is Czech, with mutual intelligibility between the two languages. This contributes to a high language similarity index.

3. Cross-cultural Communication Adaptability:
   - Slovakia's adaptability in cross-cultural communication is facilitated by its membership in the EU and exposure to multiple languages and cultures through trade and tourism.

Environmental Factors (Env)

1. Environmental Performance Index:
   - In 2022, Slovakia scored 67.5 on the Environmental Performance Index, ranking 36th globally, indicating relatively strong environmental policies and performance.

2. Carbon Footprint per Capita:
   - Slovakia's carbon footprint was approximately 6.3 metric tons per capita in 2022.

3. Renewable Energy Usage Percentage:
   - Renewable energy accounted for about 18.7% of Slovakia's total energy consumption in 2022.
