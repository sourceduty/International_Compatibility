2. Trade Openness Index:
   Croatia is integrated into global trade through its EU membership, which facilitates significant trade flows. The country has a trade openness index reflecting high engagement in international trade.

3. Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) Inflows:
   Croatia has attracted significant FDI, especially in sectors like tourism, real estate, and energy. Recent data shows FDI inflows of around $1.3 billion USD annually.
