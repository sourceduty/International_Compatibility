
Political Factors (P)
- International agreements participation:
  Chad is a participant in numerous international organizations such as the African Union (AU), United Nations (UN), International Monetary Fund (IMF), World Trade Organization (WTO), and the Paris Agreement on climate change.

- Political stability index:
  According to the World Bank, Chad's political stability and absence of violence/terrorism index was -2.21 in 2022 (on a scale from -2.5 to 2.5).

- Corruption perception index:
  Transparency International's Corruption Perceptions Index ranked Chad 162 out of 180 countries in 2022, indicating a high level of perceived corruption.

Economic Factors (E)
- GDP per capita:
  As of 2022, Chad's GDP per capita was approximately $700 (current US dollars).

- Trade openness index:
  Chad's trade as a percentage of GDP was around 61.4% in 2022, reflecting moderate trade openness.

- Foreign direct investment (FDI) inflows:
  Chad received USD 614 million in FDI inflows in 2022.

Social Factors (S)
- Education index:
  Chad's Education Index score was 0.289 in the 2022 Human Development Report, reflecting significant challenges in the education sector.

- Healthcare quality index:
  The World Health Organization (WHO) scores Chad's healthcare system as one of the lowest globally, with limited access to quality healthcare services.

- Human development index (HDI):
  Chad's HDI was 0.398 in 2022, placing it among the lowest globally.

Cultural Factors (C)
- Cultural proximity index:
  Hofstede's cultural dimensions indicate Chad has high power distance and collectivism, which influence business and social interactions significantly.

- Language similarity index:
  French and Arabic are the official languages, with numerous local languages spoken, reflecting a diverse linguistic landscape.

- Cross-cultural communication adaptability:
  Adaptability is influenced by traditional practices and the mix of ethnic groups, requiring a nuanced understanding of local customs for effective communication.

Environmental Factors (Env)
- Environmental performance index:
  Chad ranked 174 out of 180 countries in the 2022 Environmental Performance Index, highlighting severe environmental challenges.

- Carbon footprint per capita:
  Chad's carbon footprint per capita is very low due to limited industrial activity and reliance on traditional biomass for energy.

- Renewable energy usage percentage:
  Renewable energy accounts for a significant portion of energy consumption, primarily through traditional biomass and some hydroelectric power.
