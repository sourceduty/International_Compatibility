
Political Factors (P)

International Agreements Participation
- Active participant in numerous international agreements and organizations. Recently opened negotiations with the EU and signed a framework agreement with Zambia.

Political Stability Index
- Known for high political stability supported by robust democratic governance.

Corruption Perception Index
- Among the least corrupt countries globally, with a strong legal framework and transparent governance.

Economic Factors (E)

GDP per Capita
- Approximately $86,000 USD.

Trade Openness Index
- Highly open to international trade, benefiting from numerous free trade agreements.

Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) Inflows
- Attracts substantial FDI, particularly in financial services, pharmaceuticals, and technology.

Social Factors (S)

Education Index
- High education index with a strong emphasis on quality education and research.

Healthcare Quality Index
- High-quality healthcare system with excellent outcomes.

Human Development Index (HDI)
- Very high HDI, reflecting excellent outcomes in health, education, and income.

Cultural Factors (C)

Cultural Proximity Index (e.g., Hofstede's Cultural Dimensions)
- Low power distance, high individualism, and high uncertainty avoidance.

Language Similarity Index
- Four national languages (German, French, Italian, Romansh) facilitating communication and cultural exchange.

Cross-Cultural Communication Adaptability
- High adaptability due to diverse population and strong tradition of international cooperation.

Environmental Factors (Env)

Environmental Performance Index (EPI)
- Leader in environmental performance, excelling in air quality, biodiversity, and sustainability.

Carbon Footprint per Capita
- Moderate carbon footprint for a developed country.

Renewable Energy Usage Percentage
- Significant portion of energy from hydroelectric power, with growing investments in solar and wind energy.
