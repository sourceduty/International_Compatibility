
Political Factors (P)
- International agreements participation: France is an active participant in numerous international agreements, including the Paris Agreement, the Artemis Accords for space exploration, and various EU trade agreements.
- Political stability index: France's political stability index as of the latest data is 0.05 (on a scale from -2.5 to 2.5), indicating moderate stability.
- Corruption perception index: France scores 71 out of 100 on the Corruption Perceptions Index 2023, ranking 23rd globally.

Economic Factors (E)
- GDP per capita: The GDP per capita for France in 2023 is approximately $44,800 USD.
- Trade openness index: France has a trade openness index score of 57.3, reflecting the country's substantial integration into the global economy.
- Foreign direct investment (FDI) inflows: In 2023, France attracted around $37 billion USD in FDI inflows.

Social Factors (S)
- Education index: France's education index is 0.847, ranking high on the UN's Education Index due to its comprehensive education system.
- Healthcare quality index: France scores 84.7 on the Healthcare Quality and Access (HAQ) Index, making it one of the highest-ranking countries for healthcare quality.
- Human development index (HDI): France's HDI is 0.901, placing it in the very high human development category.

Cultural Factors (C)
- Cultural proximity index: Based on Hofstede's cultural dimensions, France exhibits high individualism and uncertainty avoidance, reflecting a culture that values independence and structured environments.
- Language similarity index: French is a Romance language, sharing significant lexical similarities with other Romance languages like Italian and Spanish.
- Cross-cultural communication adaptability: France scores relatively high in adaptability in cross-cultural communication, due to its diverse population and extensive international interactions.

Environmental Factors (Env)
- Environmental performance index: France ranks 5th globally with a score of 80.0 on the Environmental Performance Index, indicating strong performance in sustainability and environmental health.
- Carbon footprint per capita: France's carbon footprint per capita is approximately 4.6 metric tons of CO2 annually, lower than the global average.
- Renewable energy usage percentage: As of 2023, 23% of France's energy consumption comes from renewable sources.
