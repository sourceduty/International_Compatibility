
Political Factors (P)

International Agreements Participation
- Active participant in WTO, CARICOM, and various environmental agreements like the Paris Agreement.

Political Stability Index
- Relatively stable political environment with ongoing governance challenges.

Corruption Perception Index
- Corruption remains a significant issue, impacting various sectors.

Economic Factors (E)

GDP per Capita
- Approximately $5,600 USD.

Trade Openness Index
- Moderately open to international trade, benefiting from CARICOM membership and global trade agreements.

Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) Inflows
- Significant FDI, especially in mining and energy sectors.

Social Factors (S)

Education Index
- High literacy rate with strong commitment to improving educational outcomes.

Healthcare Quality Index
- Improving healthcare quality and accessibility with government and international support.

Human Development Index (HDI)
- Moderate ranking, with ongoing efforts to address socio-economic disparities.

Cultural Factors (C)

Cultural Proximity Index (e.g., Hofstede's Cultural Dimensions)
- High level of collectivism and moderate power distance.

Language Similarity Index
- Dutch is the official language, shared with the Netherlands and parts of the Caribbean.

Cross-Cultural Communication Adaptability
- High adaptability due to diverse ethnic and cultural composition.

Environmental Factors (Env)

Environmental Performance Index (EPI)
- Strong performance in forest conservation and biodiversity.

Carbon Footprint per Capita
- Relatively low carbon footprint.

Renewable Energy Usage Percentage
- Increasing focus on renewable energy, particularly hydroelectric power.
