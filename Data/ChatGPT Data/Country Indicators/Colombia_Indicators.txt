
Political Factors (P)
1. International Agreements Participation:
   - Colombia is a participant in numerous international agreements, including 17 Free Trade Agreements with countries such as the United States, Canada, the European Union, and members of the Pacific Alliance.
   - Colombia is also a signatory to the Paris Agreement, aimed at combating climate change.

2. Political Stability Index:
   - According to the World Bank's Worldwide Governance Indicators, Colombia's political stability and absence of violence/terrorism score for 2022 is -1.15 on a scale from -2.5 (weak) to 2.5 (strong).

3. Corruption Perception Index:
   - Transparency International's Corruption Perceptions Index (CPI) for 2022 ranks Colombia 87th out of 180 countries, with a score of 39 out of 100 (where 0 is highly corrupt and 100 is very clean).

Social Factors (S)
1. Education Index:
   - The UNDP Human Development Report 2022 indicates Colombia's Education Index is 0.658, based on mean years of schooling (8.2 years) and expected years of schooling (14.3 years).

2. Healthcare Quality Index:
   - The Legatum Prosperity Index 2022 ranks Colombia 78th out of 167 countries in the Health pillar, reflecting access to quality healthcare services.

3. Human Development Index (HDI):
   - The UNDP Human Development Index for 2022 places Colombia at 0.752, ranking 83rd out of 189 countries.

Cultural Factors (C)
1. Cultural Proximity Index (e.g., Hofstede's cultural dimensions):
   - According to Hofstede's cultural dimensions, Colombia scores high on power distance (score of 67), indicating acceptance of hierarchical order, and low on individualism (score of 13), showing a preference for collectivism.

2. Language Similarity Index:
   - Spanish is the official language of Colombia, facilitating linguistic and cultural proximity with other Spanish-speaking nations in Latin America.

3. Cross-cultural Communication Adaptability:
   - Colombia demonstrates strong cross-cultural communication adaptability, partly due to its diverse population and the influence of various cultural heritages.

Environmental Factors (Env)
1. Environmental Performance Index:
   - The 2022 Environmental Performance Index (EPI) ranks Colombia 47th out of 180 countries with a score of 55.3 out of 100, reflecting moderate environmental health and ecosystem vitality.

2. Carbon Footprint per Capita:
   - Colombia's carbon footprint per capita was approximately 1.79 metric tons of CO2 in 2021, as reported by the Global Carbon Atlas.

3. Renewable Energy Usage Percentage:
   - According to the International Renewable Energy Agency (IRENA), renewable energy accounted for about 28% of Colombia's total final energy consumption in 2022.
