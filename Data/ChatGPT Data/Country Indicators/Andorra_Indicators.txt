
Political Factors (P)

International Agreements Participation
- Andorra has been actively engaging in international agreements, particularly with the European Union. It has a monetary agreement with the EU allowing it to use the euro and issue its own euro coins. The country is also negotiating an Association Agreement with the EU to enhance economic integration and access to the EU's internal market, which will facilitate the free movement of goods, services, capital, and people. Additionally, Andorra has signed 13 double taxation agreements (CDIs) with countries including France, Spain, Luxembourg, and Portugal to avoid tax evasion and promote international trade.

Political Stability Index
- Andorra enjoys a high level of political stability, underpinned by its unique co-principality governance system. The country is known for its peaceful political environment and effective governance, contributing to its stable and resilient economic and social systems.

Corruption Perception Index
- Corruption levels in Andorra are relatively low compared to many other countries. The government's efforts to enhance transparency and compliance with international standards, especially in the financial sector, have improved its standing in global corruption perception rankings.

Economic Factors (E)

GDP per Capita
- As of 2023, Andorra's GDP per capita is approximately $49,900, reflecting its high-income economy. The economy is primarily driven by tourism, retail, and finance sectors, with a significant influx of visitors contributing to economic activity.

Trade Openness Index
- Andorra's trade openness is facilitated by its strategic agreements with the EU and neighboring countries. The Association Agreement with the EU is expected to further enhance trade openness by integrating Andorra into the EU's internal market.

Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) Inflows
- Andorra has been successful in attracting foreign direct investment, particularly in sectors like finance, technology, and tourism. The government's reforms and international agreements have created a conducive environment for FDI, promoting economic diversification and growth.

Social Factors (S)

Education Index
- The education system in Andorra is well-developed, with a high literacy rate and significant investment in educational infrastructure. The government places a strong emphasis on multilingual education, with instruction in Catalan, Spanish, French, and English.

Healthcare Quality Index
- Andorra's healthcare system is of high quality, with comprehensive coverage and access to modern medical facilities. The country has a high life expectancy and low infant mortality rates, indicative of effective healthcare services.

Human Development Index (HDI)
- Andorra ranks highly on the Human Development Index, reflecting strong performance in health, education, and standard of living. The country continues to invest in social services to maintain and improve its HDI standing.

Cultural Factors (C)

Cultural Proximity Index
- Andorra's cultural dimensions, based on Hofstede's model, indicate a society with low power distance and high individualism, similar to other Western European countries. This cultural proximity facilitates smooth interactions with its neighbors, Spain and France.

Language Similarity Index
- Andorra's official language is Catalan, but Spanish, French, and English are also widely spoken. This linguistic diversity enhances cross-cultural communication and adaptability within the country and with international partners.

Cross-Cultural Communication Adaptability
- Andorra's high level of multilingualism and cultural diversity contributes to strong cross-cultural communication adaptability. The country's small size and strategic location between Spain and France further enhance its ability to engage effectively with different cultures.

Environmental Factors (Env)

Environmental Performance Index
- Andorra is committed to environmental sustainability, with efforts to improve waste management, water quality, and renewable energy usage. The country collaborates with the EU on various environmental initiatives to enhance its performance in this area.

Carbon Footprint per Capita
- Andorra's carbon footprint per capita is moderate, with ongoing initiatives to reduce emissions and promote sustainable practices. The government is focused on increasing the use of renewable energy sources to further lower its carbon footprint.

Renewable Energy Usage Percentage
- The usage of renewable energy in Andorra is increasing, with significant investments in solar and hydroelectric power. The government's commitment to sustainability is reflected in its policies to promote clean energy and reduce reliance on fossil fuels.
