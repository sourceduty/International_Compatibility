
Political Factors (P)

International Agreements Participation
- Argentina is a member of the Southern Common Market (MERCOSUR), which includes Argentina, Brazil, Paraguay, and Uruguay, with Bolivia as a pending member. Argentina also has bilateral agreements with numerous countries, including recent agreements with the United States to enhance international tax compliance and peaceful uses of outer space.

Political Stability Index
- Argentina has faced challenges related to political stability, particularly with recent changes under the new administration of President Javier Milei. His administration has initiated significant economic reforms aimed at reducing government intervention and liberalizing the economy.

Corruption Perception Index
- Corruption remains a significant issue in Argentina. Transparency International's Corruption Perception Index ranks Argentina relatively low, indicating ongoing challenges with corruption in both public and private sectors.

Economic Factors (E)

GDP per Capita
- As of 2023, Argentina's GDP per capita is approximately $10,633. The economy is diverse, with significant contributions from agriculture, manufacturing, and services sectors.

Trade Openness Index
- Argentina has a moderate trade openness index, influenced by its membership in MERCOSUR and other trade agreements. The country engages in substantial trade with key partners like Brazil, China, and the United States.

Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) Inflows
- Argentina continues to attract FDI, particularly in sectors such as energy, mining, and agriculture. However, political and economic uncertainties have impacted the overall investment climate.

Social Factors (S)

Education Index
- Argentina has a high literacy rate and a well-developed education system, with significant investments in primary, secondary, and higher education. The country emphasizes education as a key driver of social and economic development.

Healthcare Quality Index
- The healthcare system in Argentina is robust, with extensive public and private healthcare services available. Despite economic challenges, the country maintains a relatively high standard of healthcare.

Human Development Index (HDI)
- Argentina ranks high on the Human Development Index, reflecting strong performance in health, education, and standard of living. The country continues to make strides in improving social services and economic opportunities for its population.

Cultural Factors (C)

Cultural Proximity Index
- Argentina's cultural dimensions, influenced by European immigration, reflect a society with high individualism and relatively low power distance. This cultural mix affects social behaviors and organizational dynamics within the country.

Language Similarity Index
- The official language of Argentina is Spanish, which facilitates communication and cultural exchange with other Spanish-speaking countries. This linguistic similarity supports the country's adaptability in international relations and business.

Cross-Cultural Communication Adaptability
- Argentina's diverse cultural heritage and high level of education contribute to a strong adaptability in cross-cultural communication. The country's population is well-versed in multiple languages and cultural contexts, enhancing its ability to engage effectively in global interactions.

Environmental Factors (Env)

Environmental Performance Index
- Argentina faces several environmental challenges, including deforestation, pollution, and climate change impacts. The government is actively working on improving environmental sustainability through various initiatives and international cooperation.

Carbon Footprint per Capita
- Argentina's carbon footprint per capita is relatively high due to its industrial activities and energy consumption patterns. Efforts are being made to increase the use of renewable energy sources and reduce emissions.

Renewable Energy Usage Percentage
- The usage of renewable energy in Argentina is increasing, with significant investments in wind, solar, and bioenergy projects. These efforts aim to diversify the energy mix and reduce reliance on fossil fuels, contributing to environmental sustainability.
